OUR MOTIVATION

The current situation we are stuck in, are the ones we never thought we would face. Everything has changed and the work from home era has started for the working adults and teenagers are being taught through online classes but there is very less stress laid on what we are doing for the new generation, the toddlers. As we know the correct age to start learning is around 3-4 years but now in these situations we cannot make a toddler go to school or sit for an online class, so what can we do to make their brains start developing at the age it should ?

That’s what made us take up this project, The toddler’s class. We are trying to make an interactive and fun learning platform where toddlers can actually learn while being safe.

What are the advantages of practicing maths online?

Convenience and flexibility are the most common advantages of our website. It is ideal for someone of whom we never know when they are in mood of studying like your child. Also, Our website allow you to fit school into your child's schedule easily.

How much time child should spend every day?

The idea of this teaching system is solely based on your child's interest and capabilities. They can study as much as they can

Is it Free?

As this is an initiative for the betterment of children, we have decided not to take any money as of now, but it may change if people require more services.

Should the kid know anything prior to using the website?

You'll require a desktop/tablet/phone with an active internet connection.

How will I be graded?

Online learning is very similar to an on-campus class experience. The criteria for grading will be established by our system as the machine will learn to reward points.

What if there is a fault in grading by the system?

As this is an initiative from our side and we are not charging any amount of money, so we'll request you to give your valuable feedback by writing us an e-mail in case you find any fault or bug so that we can make amends and bring out a better product.
